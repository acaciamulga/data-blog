Here I present a table of occurrence density by country.

  1. Country/Territory can be any region with an iso-2 code. Only countries larger than 10,000 km2 are shown.
  2. Density is the total number of occurrence records occurring inside the country (Note this does not mean the record was necessarily published by the country).
  3. Change (2017) is the change in density since 2017.
  4. Non-bird density is the total density which is not coming from class Aves (i.e. all Plants, Insects, Fungi, other Animals …). Quite a few countries have very low non-bird density, meaning most of their occurrence records are birds (see the United States).
  5. Records column is the total number of occurrence records on GBIF occurring inside the country (again not necessarily published by the country).
  • For a csv of this table with more columns you can go here.
